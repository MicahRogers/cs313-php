The Team


Andrew Larsen – programmer, head of marketing, and project manager

Andrew has always dreamed of owning his own business. His experience in software development and love of video games motivated him to start Citadel Games and form this team. His go-to games are Pokémon, The Legend of Zelda, and anything with Mario in it.


Micah Rogers – lead programmer

Micah started game development as part of his education as a software engineer. When he isn’t crushing everyone at Super Smash Bros, he spends his time squashing programming bugs and complaining about how lame writing is. He believes that Metroid Prime and Luigi’s Mansion are two of the most beautifully crafted games ever.


Roger Pollard – music producer, web developer

Roger has been writing music since he was a child. Some of his favorite pieces have been found on the soundtrack of games developed by Nintendo. Beginning at an early age, he started transcribing his favorite songs from games in the Animal Crossing, Pokémon, and Mario franchises. After spending a couple years writing music for his YouTube channel, he moved over to producing music for video games.
